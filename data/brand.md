# Brand Voice (Mock)

Voice attributes:
- Professional: clear, confident, concise
- Playful: light wit, no sarcasm, no slang overload
- Enterprise: credible, specific, avoids hype

Do:
- Lead with outcomes and operational impact
- Use numbers when available
- Keep sentences short

Do not:
- Overpromise or claim guarantees
- Use aggressive urgency ("act now", "last chance")
- Mention competitors by name

Formatting:
- Headlines under 60 characters
- CTA is a simple verb phrase
