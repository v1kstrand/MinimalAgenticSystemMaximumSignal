# Product Brief (Eval)

Product: PulseDesk
Category: SaaS helpdesk for subscription businesses
Summary: Unifies ticketing, chat, and customer history so support teams resolve issues faster.

Audience:
- Customer support leads at B2B SaaS
- CX managers at subscription brands

Value Props:
- One inbox for email, chat, and in-app tickets
- Customer timeline shows recent activity and plan status
- SLA alerts keep response times on track

Proof Points:
- "Reduced average response time by 35%" (beta customers)
- "Cut onboarding time for new agents to 3 days" (ops report)

Secondary CTA: View the support playbook
